Privacy Policy for FlowHub

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

FlowHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our task management application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Profile picture
  • Google Account ID

2.2 Gmail Data

With your explicit permission, we access your Gmail to:

  • Read email subjects, sender information, and content to identify actionable tasks
  • Convert emails into tasks based on AI analysis
  • Display email notifications within the application

We only read your emails - we never send, delete, or modify them.

2.3 Google Calendar Data

With your permission, we access your Google Calendar to:

  • Create calendar events for tasks with deadlines
  • Read existing calendar events to avoid scheduling conflicts
  • Update task-related calendar events

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the FlowHub service
  • Convert emails into actionable tasks using AI
  • Schedule tasks and create calendar events
  • Send task reminders and notifications
  • Improve and optimize our service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Encrypted database connections
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • Industry-standard security practices

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We only share your information:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who assist in operating our application (e.g., Google Gemini AI for task extraction)

6. Third-Party Services

FlowHub uses the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs: For Gmail and Calendar integration
  • Google Gemini AI: For analyzing emails and extracting tasks

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your Gmail and Calendar at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Export your data

8. Children's Privacy

FlowHub is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

FlowHub's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
